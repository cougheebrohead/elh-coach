Every feature shipped

The tools your clients actually use.

Not feature-creep. Not a kitchen sink. The things every serious coach asks for, built right and shipping today.

— 01 / Nutrition

AI photo meal recognition

Snap a plate, get every macro. Multi-item plates, sauces, sides — parsed and cross-referenced against the USDA food database. Powered by Gemini Flash with OpenAI Vision fallback.

— 02 / Nutrition

Barcode scan w/ USDA fallback

Open Food Facts as primary, USDA SR + FoodData Central as fallback. GTIN checksum validation. The barcode scanner that actually finds the food at every grocery store, not just Whole Foods.

— 03 / Female-specific

Cycle-aware coaching

Phase-aware tips (menstrual / follicular / ovulation / luteal), calorie targets that move with hormones, training adjustments by phase. Built into the app, not bolted on as an afterthought.

— 04 / Daily readiness

Recovery / readiness score

Sleep + HRV + load → today's readiness score (0–100). Gives you the right context to write programming for tomorrow without asking "how do you feel?" twenty times.

— 05 / Glucose

Time-in-Range + GMI

Continuous glucose tracking with Time-in-Range %, estimated A1C (GMI), low/high event counts, 14-day trend. For coaches working metabolic health, GLP-1, or pre-diabetic populations.

— 06 / Real-time

In-app messaging

One thread per client, with read receipts. The last place your clients should be messaging you is Instagram DMs. Built in, indexed by client, retrievable forever.

— 07 / Workouts

Workout programming

Programs, days, exercises with sets/reps/RPE. Drag-to-reorder, template library, assign-to-roster. Push a program update once and everyone on it sees it.

— 08 / Safety

Allergen + condition alerts

Cross-checks every meal log against client allergies, medical conditions, medications, and dietary restrictions. Flags before they hit submit.

— 09 / Money

Stripe Connect billing

Your Stripe account, your money, your control. Set the prices, run the charges, get paid directly. We don't take a cut of your transactions — ever.

Common questions

Things coaches ask before they sign up.

What happens to the $89/mo when I upgrade to my real domain?

It stops. The Real Domain Upgrade replaces the subscription — you pay $200/yr maintenance instead. We migrate your tenant to your domain in about 24 hours; existing client logins keep working through automatic redirects.

What happens after my 14-day trial ends?

You'll get a reminder three days before billing starts. If you don't add a card, your account locks until you do — your data is preserved. Cancel anytime, no friction, no clawback.

Do I have to install anything? What about my clients?

Zero install for you — the Coach Portal is a web app at yourname.elhcoach.app. Your clients can use the PWA (which installs to their home screen on iOS and Android in two taps), or you can add the optional Native Apps Add-On to publish real App Store / Play Store apps under your developer account.

What's the AI nutrition powered by?

Photo recognition uses Gemini Flash (primary) and OpenAI Vision (fallback) with a USDA cross-reference for canonical macros. Barcode scanning hits Open Food Facts first, falls back to USDA SR / FoodData Central. We validate GTIN checksums to reject typos.

Is the cycle / glucose / recovery stuff actually shipping?

Yes — every feature on this page is in production. Cycle phase tracking, today's readiness scoring, glucose Time-in-Range with estimated A1C — all live and used by coaches today. The screenshots above are the actual app rendering live data.

HIPAA / BAA?

BAA-ready on the Real Domain tier. We execute the Business Associate Agreement before any PHI flows through the platform. The base subscription tier isn't intended for clinical workflows.

Can I migrate from Trainerize / Everfit / something else?

Yes — we'll import your client roster, programs, and meal templates. The 30-min onboarding call covers the migration. Most coaches are fully switched in under a week.

Can I export my data if I leave?

Always. Full JSON + CSV export of every client, meal log, message, program, biometric. Your data is yours, on every tier.

Why is your starting price lower than the competition?

Because we want you to upgrade to the Real Domain tier. The $89/mo is the on-ramp; the $2,500 ownership upgrade is the actual business model. Trainerize and Everfit make money keeping you on a subscription forever — we make money helping you graduate off ours.

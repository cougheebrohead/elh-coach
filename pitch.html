Executive Summary
A branded coaching platform that retains the client and the trainer.

ELH Coach is a multi-tenant SaaS platform that gives independent personal trainers, dietitians, and coaching studios a fully branded mobile + web experience for their clients — powered by an AI nutrition engine that no incumbent owns at this depth.

"Trainerize made workouts portable. We make the trainer's brand portable — and add the daily nutrition layer that actually retains the client."

The Thesis in One Paragraph

Independent trainers are losing clients to ghosting because their tools don't retain attention between sessions. The dominant platforms — Trainerize, TrueCoach, Everfit — are workout-first. ELH Coach is nutrition-first, AI-powered, fully white-label, and clinical-grade. Trainers earn higher LTV, clients stay longer, and the platform compounds value with every meal logged, every cycle tracked, every program completed.

Why This Wins

  1. AI photo macros at no incremental cost to the trainer. Clients open the branded app three times a day. Nobody else delivers this in a white-label SaaS.

Section 03
Product & Platform
Three branded surfaces. One AI engine. Built for retention.

Every coach gets their own branded mobile app, web portal, and email sender. Clients see the trainer's logo, colors, and domain on every screen. The platform is invisible.

Surface 01

Coach Console

Branded dashboard for the trainer. Roster with engagement risk pills, per-client drill-down, programs library, schedule, weekly digest emails, branded client reports.

Surface 02

Client Mobile App

Daily nutrition ring, AI photo logging, current workout, message thread with the coach, progress photos, weight chart, streak. Native iOS, Android, and PWA.

Surface 03

Studio Multi-Trainer Suite

Up to five trainers per studio, custom domain, Stripe Connect for client payments, role-based access, studio-wide reports, branded onboarding flow.

Surface 04

Brand White-Label

Full white-label removal of all platform branding, API + webhooks, BAA available, dedicated CSM, native iOS/Android shells in App Store under their name.

Founder & Operating Discipline
Built by an operator who has shipped a portfolio of production-grade products.

ELH Coach is one of three products in the ELH Empire portfolio sharing a single proprietary engine — alongside FitApp consumer, the Heads Health Platform enterprise product, and Body by Chosen as the prototype customer.

Operating Principles

  1. Production-grade only. Zero-trust architecture, OWASP-hardened, cross-tenant isolation tests gate every deploy. No "MVPs," no shortcuts.
  2. Polish over speed. Externally visible work ships at the Steve Jobs bar or it doesn't ship. Empty states, error messages, loading states — all designed.
  3. Compounding engine, multiple distribution channels. The shared engine (fitapp-core) powers consumer, trainer SaaS, and enterprise products simultaneously. Every improvement compounds across the portfolio.
  4. Operator-owned go-to-market. Direct trainer outreach, influencer partnerships, conference circuit. No paid-acquisition treadmill.

Risk Analysis
Honest assessment of what could go wrong — and how we mitigate.
RiskLikelihoodMitigation
AI cost outpaces revenue at scaleMediumSelf-hosted Llama 3.1 70B inference path documented. Margin expands to 90%+ once free-tier traffic shifts to in-house GPU.
Trainer churn higher than projectedMediumAnnual prepay (2 months free) cuts churn 60-80%. Sticky multi-client lock-in compounds. Coach-tier conversion to Studio tier reduces churn further.
Incumbent (Trainerize) ships AI nutritionLow18-24 month head start. White-label remains our differentiator even if AI parity reached. Switching cost is the trainer's branded URL + client roster.
Apple App Store policy changesLowPWA covers any policy gaps. Brand-tier native shells signed under each customer's developer account.
Concentration in one coach segmentLowPricing tiers + adjacent verticals (dietitians, RDs, GLP-1) diversify the customer base by design.
HIPAA scope expansion forces compliance workMediumBAA architecture in place from day one. Brand-tier customers absorb the certification cost via their tier pricing.
Founder bandwidth at scaleHighFirst operator hire scoped at $20k MRR (CSM/support contractor). First engineer at $50k MRR. Roadmap sequenced to delay engineering hires.
Worst-case path: If both AI cost and trainer churn run worse than projected, the conservative scenario still produces $648k ARR and breakeven operations. The platform is engineered to be fundable from itself even in a downside case.

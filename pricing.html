Frequently asked

Can I switch plans?

Yes — anytime, from the billing portal. Upgrades take effect immediately and prorate; downgrades apply at the next renewal.

What happens after my trial ends?

You'll get a reminder 3 days before billing starts. If you don't cancel, you'll be charged for whichever plan you selected at signup. Cancel anytime — no questions asked.

Do you offer HIPAA / BAA?

Yes, on the Brand tier. Contact us to set up the Business Associate Agreement before sending any PHI through the platform.

What if I exceed my client limit?

You'll get a soft warning. We never auto-charge or auto-upgrade. We'll reach out to discuss the right plan for your scale.

Can I export my data if I leave?

Always. JSON + CSV export of every client, meal log, and message at any time, no questions asked. Your data is yours.

Do you have a referral or partner program?

Yes — contact partnerships@elhcoachhq.app. We pay 20% recurring for the first year of every account you refer.

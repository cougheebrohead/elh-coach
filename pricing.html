Frequently asked

Can I move up a tier later?

Yes — upgrade in the billing portal at any time. Subdomain → custom domain takes us about 24h to migrate; existing client logins keep working through automatic redirects.

What happens after my trial ends?

You'll get a reminder three days before billing starts. If you don't cancel, you'll be charged for whichever plan you signed up under. Cancel anytime, no friction.

HIPAA / BAA?

Custom Domain tier only. We execute the Business Associate Agreement before any PHI flows through the platform.

What if I exceed my client limit?

Soft warning, never an auto-charge. We'll reach out and have a real conversation about whether the next tier fits.

Can I export my data if I leave?

Always. JSON + CSV export of every client, meal log, message, program, biometric. Your data is yours.

Referral / partner program?

Yes — partnerships@elhcoach.app. 20% recurring for the first year of every account you refer.

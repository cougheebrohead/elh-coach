Frequently asked

What happens to the $89/mo when I upgrade to my real domain?

It stops. The Real Domain Upgrade replaces the subscription — you pay $200/yr maintenance instead. We migrate your tenant to your domain in about 24 hours; existing client logins keep working through automatic redirects.

What happens after my trial ends?

You'll get a reminder three days before billing starts. If you don't add a card, your account locks until you do. Cancel anytime, no friction, no clawback.

HIPAA / BAA?

BAA-ready on the Real Domain tier. We execute the Business Associate Agreement before any PHI flows through the platform.

What if I exceed 25 clients on the subscription?

Soft warning, never an auto-charge. We'll reach out and have a real conversation about whether the Real Domain Upgrade fits — that's where unlimited clients live.

Do I have to buy the native apps?

No. Most coaches start on the PWA (which installs to the home screen) and add native apps when their book of business justifies the App Store / Play Store presence. Available at any tier.

Can I export my data if I leave?

Always. JSON + CSV export of every client, meal log, message, program, biometric. Your data is yours, on every tier.

Referral / partner program?

Yes — partnerships@elhcoach.app. 20% recurring for the first year of every account you refer.
